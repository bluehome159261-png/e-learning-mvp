The Reality

From Challenges to Solutions

Young athletes in Cameroon face real, structural barriers. RIS was built specifically to break each one of them.

The Challenges

The Kudo Trap

Earning ~1,000 CFA per informal match with no financial security or career growth

Sports Betting Dependency

Platforms like 1xBet exploit financial desperation, deepening instability

Digital Skills Gap

Less than 25% of African youth acquire market-relevant digital skills (UNESCO, 2022)

No Flexible Training

Rigid school schedules conflict with competition calendars and training routines

RIS Solutions

Mentor-Led Learning

Real industry professionals guide you live, not pre-recorded videos you watch alone

Mobile-First Platform

Designed for Cameroonian smartphones — lightweight and works on slow connections

Athlete-Friendly Schedule

Classes available 6AM–10PM GMT+1 to fit around training and competitions

Certified Career Skills

Earn verifiable certificates in web development, design, agriculture, business, and more

Why RIS

What Makes RIS Different

Not another generic eLearning platform. Built specifically for young people in Cameroon.

Live Screen Sharing

Mentors demonstrate tasks live. With your consent, they can see your screen and fix problems in real time — not a pre-recorded video.

Africa-First Pricing

Pay in XOF with plans starting at 5,000 CFA/month. Scholarships available for athletes who cannot afford any subscription.

Works on Slow Data

Designed to load fast on affordable smartphones. No heavy downloads, no wasted data — built for Cameroon's real network reality.

Bilingual Platform

All content available in both French and English — because Cameroon speaks both, and so does RIS.

Built Around Your Schedule

Classes from 6AM to 10PM GMT+1. Train in the morning, learn in the evening. No rigid timetables, no missed sessions.

Real Community

Connect with peers, share progress, ask questions. Not a lonely library — a genuine support network of athletes learning together.
